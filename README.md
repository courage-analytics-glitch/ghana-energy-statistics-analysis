# Ghana Energy Statistics Dashboard (2000-2022)

A Power BI dashboard commissioned for board-level reporting, analyzing Ghana's energy sector performance across 22 years of data from the Ministry of Energy.

---

## Project Overview

This dashboard covers Ghana's energy landscape from 2000 to 2022, providing decision-makers with a clear view of petroleum consumption, electricity generation, and renewable energy trends.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Petroleum Sector Overview
- Total petroleum consumption by product type
- Year-on-year consumption trends (2000-2022)
- Top consumed petroleum products by volume

### Page 2: Electricity Generation
- Total electricity generation by source (thermal, hydro, renewable)
- Generation capacity vs. actual output
- Annual growth in electricity production

### Page 3: Renewable Energy Trends
- Renewable energy share of total generation
- Solar and wind contribution over time
- Progress toward national energy targets

### Page 4: Energy Supply and Demand
- Total primary energy supply by source
- Energy import and export balances
- Supply gap analysis by year

### Page 5: Key Metrics Summary
- KPI cards for total energy consumption, peak generation, and renewable share
- Year-over-year comparison table
- Executive summary visuals for board presentation

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel (data cleaning and preparation)
- Data Source: Ghana Ministry of Energy, Energy Statistics 2000-2022
